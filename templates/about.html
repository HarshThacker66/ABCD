About Hotel Hakaji

Discover our story, heritage, and commitment to exceptional hospitality

Our Story

Established in 1995, Hotel Hakaji has been a beacon of authentic Indian hospitality in the heart of Bhuj, Kachchh. Named after the legendary Hakaji, a revered figure in local folklore known for his generosity and warmth, our hotel embodies the timeless values of Gujarati culture.

Located strategically near Aiya Nagar on Mundra Road, we serve as the perfect gateway to explore the rich cultural heritage of Kachchh, including its famous handicrafts, traditional architecture, and the pristine beauty of the Rann of Kachchh. Our commitment to preserving local traditions while providing modern comforts has made us a preferred destination for travelers seeking an authentic Gujarati experience.

Over the years, we have welcomed thousands of guests from around the world, each carrying with them memories of our warm hospitality, traditional cuisine, and the genuine care that defines the Hakaji experience.

Our Values
